                    K-pop group ITZY show off their playful side in new music video CHESHIRE. Watch
                    Bhavna Agarwal
                    Mumbai,UPDATED: Nov 30, 2022 18:06 IST

                    In Short

                    • ITZY released their sixth mini-album, CHESHIRE today, November 30.
                    • They also released the music video for the title track.
                    • ITZY is a five-members group under JYP Entertainment.

                    K-pop group ITZY returns with new music. The five-member JYP group released the music video for CHESHIRE along with the mini-album. The new video shows the ITZY members, Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong and Yuna, being seen on a stage set of a reality game show where they deliver playful choreography with bold moves.

                    CHESHIRE MUSIC VIDEO

                    The pop dance song is light and asks everyone to believe in themselves. “Hey, why so serious? / Just trust what you see / Don’t complicate it, nothing’s wrong, right? / Can you see me? Hey, why so curious? / Trust your gut, not your logic / Just take it easy, anything’s right,” the girls sing in the chorus. The song was dropped today, November 30, at 6 PM KST (2:30 PM IST). Fans are already showering it with love.

                    advertisement

                    ABOUT THE MINI ALBUM

                    Apart from the music video for ‘Cheshire’, ITZY’s sixth mini-album also includes three B-sides — ‘Snowy’, Freaky’, and their English track, Boys Like You.

                    The K-pop group, ITZY, recently concluded the North American leg of their world tour titled ‘Checkmate’ on November 13 in New York. The quintet will resume their tour to Southeast Asian cities like Jakarta, Manila, Singapore and Bangkok in early 2023, after promoting Cheshire.
